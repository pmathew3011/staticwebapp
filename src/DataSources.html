Phishing

Phishing is the leading cause of healthcare data breaches worldwide​. Attacks are rapidly increasing in number and sophistication​. It is estimated that 40% of cyberattacks begin with phishing​. According to a 2022 report, 2.9% of phishing emails are clicked​. Phishing is used to steal credentials which allows for unauthorized access to​ deploy malware​, establish advanced persistent threat​, directly steal/compromise data​.

Ransomware

Ransomware is a type of malware that holds a victim's data or device hostage, threatening to keep it locked—or worse—unless the victim pays a ransom to the attacker​. Ransomware attacks have risen by 14% in the last 5 years, with an average cost of $1.8M per incident​. Healthcare, Government and Education are top 3 most frequently targeted industries. It takes 22 days on average to identify and remediate ransomware attacks​. Impact of ransomware attacks on Healthcare industry is the most catastrophic, they cripple key systems and prevent hospitals from accessing crucial patient data until a fee is paid​. White House reclassified ransomware as a national security threat in 2023​.
